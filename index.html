About me

Welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Prajit Giri, a passionate and driven individual with a diverse background in business, finance, and digital marketing. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Management and Finance at Fisher College, Boston, I am committed to academic excellence and continuous growth. Originally from a remote village in Sindhupalchok, Nepal, I bring a unique perspective shaped by my international experiences and cultural upbringing.

After studying for two years in Dhaka on a fully funded scholarship and transferring to Fisher College in Boston, United States with a presidential scholarship, I've acquired a strong foundation in financial analysis, strategic planning, and leadership, reflected in my cumulative GPA of 3.59 from BRAC University. My coursework has equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of business principles, complemented by practical skills in data analysis, programming, and digital marketing. Through internships and hands-on experiences, I've honed my abilities to thrive in dynamic and fast-paced environments.

Beyond academics, I've been actively involved in various leadership and community initiatives. From serving as the Student Fellowship member at the esteemed Samaanta Foundation to spearheading recruitment efforts as a Junior Executive for HR and Admin at BRAC University Entrepreneurship Development Forum, I've demonstrated a commitment to making a positive impact. Whether mentoring underprivileged students or leading digital marketing campaigns, I strive to leverage my skills and experiences to inspire change and drive meaningful outcomes.
